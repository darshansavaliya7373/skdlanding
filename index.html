About Us

SKD COMPOSITE Company manafactures FRP Rebar of 3mm to 40mm diameter with the latest technology in india We belive in providing lower cost and better quality as per customer requirements. Customer satisfaction is our Motto

Infinity Rebars specializes in the production of FRP (Glass Fibre Reinforced Polymer) rebar. Our FRP bars have high tensile strength, are 2 times stronger and 4 times lighter than steel bars.

It is now the top material for design and construction in East Africa, UAE and India.
