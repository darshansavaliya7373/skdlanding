SKD For Swimming Pool

Benefits of GFRP for Swimming Pools.

Installation is quick and easy.

The use of GFRP Rebar increases the life span of swimming pool.

Unlike steel GFRP Rebar can be provided in custom length which can help in reducing cost.

A smooth surface that is comfortable for swimmers.

Surface easier to clean.

cost effective.

Advantages of GFRP Rebar for Bridges

GFRP Rebar is 1/4 the weight of steel rebar and boasts twice the tensile strength of steel.

Increase Life Span of Bridges: Incorporating GFRP Rebar significantly enhances the lifespan of bridges, providing robust structural support.

Custom Manufacturing Options: GFRP Rebar can be manufactured in custom lengths, bends, and shapes, offering versatile solutions for various construction needs.

Cost-Effective Solution: GFRP Rebar proves to be a cost-effective alternative, delivering a balance of performance and affordability for construction projects.
