About Us

Replacement of steel rebars by GFRP rebars in the concrete structures.

Infinity Rebars is one of the world's leading manufacturers of GFRP pultrusion products. SKD Rebars are manufactured in Surat, Gujarat, Western India. We are a reference point for R&D, production, testing, fabrication and logistics support of GFRP composites.

Over the years, the company has experienced constant growth and, thanks to its history of innovation, our integrated solutions have gained worldwide respect and recognition. Infinity Rebars is actively working on the introduction of modern equipment at Russian factories. Infinity Rebars specializes in the production of GFRP (Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer) rebar.

Our GFRP rebars have high tensile strength, are 2 times stronger and 4 times lighter than steel bars. It is now the top material for design and construction in East Africa, UAE and India. Now, in just two years, Infinity Rebars has achieved the remarkable feat of becoming India's largest manufacturer and supplier of GFRP rebars despite the pandemic.
